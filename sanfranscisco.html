San Franscisco, CA

Last semester, I joined the Silicon Valley Program and lived in San Francisco. Since we were not on campus and did not have dining halls, I had to learn how to cook.

I'm not a huge fan of cooking, in fact I find it stressful rather than relaxing. As a result, I only cooked easy things such as pasta or avocado toast. Since my favorite cuisine is Italian, I ended up eating a lot of pasta.

However, one of my roommates loved cooking and soon took over the menu. We ended up cooking some really great beyond burgers,fried rice, buttered carrots, and stir fry.
