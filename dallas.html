Dallas

Once my office went to work from home mode, I moved to Dallas to live with some of cousins for a few weeks. In fact, I lived with two separate cousins in Dallas and had different food experinces in both.

In the first house I lived, my cousins absolutely loved cooking. Most distinctively, they made the best homemade pizza I have ever had. In fact, it was a Thursday night ritual to make pizza. This pizza was the perfect combination of Trader Joe's bread, homemade sauce, and sharp cheddar cheese.

In the second house I lived in, I enjoyed having good homecooked Indian food and paninis. I also got to make boxed brownies every weekend, which was amazing.
