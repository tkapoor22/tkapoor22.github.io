Tanya's 2020 Food Diary

This year has taken a turn none of us have expected. However, I like to think of myself as an optimistic person and someone who prefers to look at the silver lining of each challenge. Over the course of the last few months, I have lived in three different cities and this website will document some of my favorite meals in each city.

However, before I take you all to the different cities, here's a little bit about me. I'm currenly a junior in college and am taking all of my classes online. I'm a huge foodie, as shown by the theme of this website, and my favorite cuisine is Italian. I also have a really sweet tooth and favorite dessert has to be brownies, especially the boxed kind
